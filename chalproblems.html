Challenging Problem Presentations—Time to Shine!

Pick a problem from anywhere—departure passes, problem sets, exams, or anything else you find interesting. Once you’ve picked your problem, sign up for a presentation date on the sheet and list your name and the problem.

Heads up:

Check what problems your classmates have already picked. If someone else has your problem, you can either:

  • Team up with them (max 2 people per team)
  • Pick a different problem

Presentation Tips:

  • Make slides (PowerPoint, Google Slides, or LaTeX/Beamer—LaTeX/Beamer gives an extra point!)
  • Explain the problem and your solution clearly
  • Keep it short—about 5 minutes

Grading:

  • Correctness – 5 pts
  • Clarity – 3 pts
  • Slide design/layout – 1 pt
  • Depth of explanation – 1 pt
