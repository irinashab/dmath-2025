Mastery-Based Grading System

A learning-focused approach that values growth and understanding

🎯Level 3: Mastery
You've mastered the material

Demonstrated by:

  • Complete understanding of core concepts
  • Accurate application of principles
  • Clear and precise explanations
  • Effective problem-solving strategies
  • Consistent high-quality work

What it means:

  • You've met all learning objectives
  • You can apply knowledge to new situations
  • No significant gaps in understanding
🚧
Level 2: Progressing
Almost there - needs refinement

Demonstrated by:

  • Solid understanding with minor gaps
  • Most concepts applied correctly
  • Small errors in execution
  • Good effort with room for improvement

🔄 Pathway to Mastery

You can upgrade to Level 3 by:

  • Submitting improved work within 1 week
  • Meeting briefly during office hours
  • Demonstrating understanding of feedback
  • Correcting specific identified issues
🌱
Level 1: Developing
Needs significant work

Demonstrated by:

  • Fundamental misunderstandings
  • Inconsistent application of concepts
  • Multiple significant errors
  • Incomplete or missing work

Next Steps:

  • Required office hours visit
  • Revision opportunity after consultation
  • Additional practice resources provided
  • Personalized learning plan
